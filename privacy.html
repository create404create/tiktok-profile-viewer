🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 6, 2026

Information We Collect

We DO NOT collect any personal information. This app runs entirely in your browser.

How We Use Data

When you enter a TikTok username, we fetch public profile information directly from TikTok's Display API. No data is stored on our servers. All processing happens in real-time and is discarded after you close the page.

What Information We Access

  • Public TikTok username (provided by you)
  • Public display name
  • Public follower count
  • Public following count
  • Public bio/signature
  • Public profile picture URL

Third-Party Services

This app uses TikTok's Display API. Please refer to TikTok's privacy policy for more information: TikTok Privacy Policy

Data Storage

No data is stored. We do not use cookies, local storage, or any databases.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
